Introduction
libcg is a library that abstracts the control group file system in Linux.

What are Control Groups
Control Groups provide a mechanism for aggregating/partitioning sets of tasks, and all their future children, into hierarchical groups with specialized behaviour [1]. 
It makes use of a filesystem interface.
